### **📌 Summary of Model Development Process**  

1️⃣ **Started with Two Models:**  
   - You initially asked for two different ML models.  
   - We decided to focus on an **autoencoder-based anomaly detection model**.  

2️⃣ **Dataset Transition:**  
   - The original dataset was too large, leading to memory issues.  
   - We switched to the **KDD dataset (NSL-KDD)** for network anomaly detection.  

3️⃣ **Basic Autoencoder Implementation:**  
   - Built an autoencoder with a **simple three-layer structure**.  
   - The model struggled with **low recall for anomalies** and overfitting.  

4️⃣ **Incremental Improvements:**  
   - **Lowered anomaly detection threshold** (from 95% percentile → 90% → IQR method).  
   - **Added noise to training data** to improve generalization.  
   - **Balanced the dataset** by undersampling normal data.  

5️⃣ **Advanced Optimizations:**  
   - Increased model **complexity** by **adding more neurons** and depth.  
   - Switched activation functions to **LeakyReLU and SELU** for better learning.  
   - Changed **batch size and epochs** for better convergence.  
   - Introduced **Isolation Forest** as a hybrid approach.  

6️⃣ **Final Results:**  
   - **Test Accuracy: 60.97%** (highest achieved).  
   - **Recall for anomalies improved from 18% → 39%**.  
   - **False negatives reduced significantly.**  

This final model **performs significantly better** than the initial implementation. 🚀
